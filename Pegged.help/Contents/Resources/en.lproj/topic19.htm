Rules of the Game

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Pegged Rules:

1 The playing area is a plus-shaped grid of holes. At the beginning of each game, there is a configuration of pegs in the area.

A hole.


A peg.

2 You can remove a peg by jumping another peg over it. Only certain jumps are legal: You must jump horizontally or vertically, and you can only jump one peg at a time.

3 The game is over when no legal jumps are available. If there is only one peg remaining, you win.
